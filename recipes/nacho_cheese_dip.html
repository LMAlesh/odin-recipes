Ragu alla Bolognese

    Ingredients
  • 25 g Flour
  • 30 g Butter
  • 150 g Cheddar Cheese
  • 100 g Cheese of choice
  • 300 ml Milk
  • 1/2 tbsp Salt
  • 1/2 tbsp Sweet Red Pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Chilli Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Shallot
  • 1/2 cup Jalapeño
    Steps (mix at all times)
  1. In a pan, melt the butter
  2. When water evaporates, add Flour
  3. When the smell of raw flour dissipates, slowly add milk
  4. mix rigorously while adding milk
  5. Upon densification, start adding the cheese
  6. Add Jalapeños
  7. Season and serve
