Ragu alla Bolognese

    Ingredients
  • 1/2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 300 g Ground Beef
  • 150 g Pancetta
  • 50 g Carrot
  • 50 g Celery
  • 50 g Onion
  • 1 cube Chicken Stock
  • 120 ml White Wine
  • 300 g Tomato Passata
  • 1 cup (240 ml) full fat milk
  • 500 g Pasta
    Steps
  1. Chop Celery, Carrot and Onion
  2. Cook the Carrots for 5 min on an oiled pan
  3. Cook the pasta
  4. Put the rest of chopped vegetables in the pan
  5. Cook untill soft
  6. Put the cooked vegetables aside
  7. In an another pan, fry the meat in a little bit of olive oil
  8. Deglaze the meat with white wine, slowly
  9. Mix in the vegetables
  10. Add the tomato passata with a cube of chicken stock
  11. Mix everything together
  12. Season and cook on low fire for at least 1 hour
  13. Serve
